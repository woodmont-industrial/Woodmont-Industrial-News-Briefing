Woodmont Properties

2025 Technology Year in Review | AI & PropTech Initiatives

Executive Summary

In 2025, Woodmont Properties deployed key AI and PropTech tools that delivered measurable improvements in operational efficiency, resident experience, and team productivity. This document highlights completed rollouts, current pilots, and 2026 strategic priorities focused on speed, accuracy, and scalability across our CRE operations.

2025 Rollouts - Delivered Business Value

Monty (EliseAI) - Leasing & Resident Communication Automation

  • Efficiency Gain: Automates leasing inquiries and resident communications 24/7, reducing response time from hours to seconds
  • Team Impact: Frees onsite teams from repetitive questions, allowing focus on relationship-building and complex resident issues
  • Consistency: Delivers uniform, accurate responses across all properties, improving brand experience and reducing communication errors
  • Scalability: Handles inquiry volume spikes without additional headcount, supporting growth without proportional staffing increases
Estimated Time Savings: 15-20 hours/week per property

Microsoft Copilot (Free & Licensed) - Enterprise Productivity

  • Drafting Speed: Accelerates document creation, email responses, and report generation across all departments
  • Data Analysis: Licensed Copilot in Excel enables faster financial analysis, trend identification, and reporting for accounting and leadership
  • Meeting Efficiency: Copilot in Teams generates summaries and action items, reducing post-meeting administrative time by 30-40%
  • Adoption: Free tier provides baseline AI literacy; licensed tier deployed where ROI is demonstrable (accounting, operations, leadership)
Average 2-3 hours saved per licensed user per week

Docutrax - Insurance Certificate & Compliance Tracking

  • Risk Reduction: Automated tracking eliminates missed renewals and expired certificates, reducing liability exposure
  • Manual Work Eliminated: Replaces spreadsheet-based tracking and manual follow-up emails with automated alerts and workflows
  • Audit Readiness: Centralized documentation ensures immediate access to compliance records for audits or incidents
  • 2025 Completion: Property Management fully implemented; Commercial/Industrial rollout in progress
Estimated 10-12 hours saved per property per month

Hootsuite - Social Media & Marketing Workflow

  • Campaign Efficiency: Centralized scheduling and posting reduces time spent managing multiple social platforms
  • Consistency: Standardized posting cadence improves brand presence and engagement across all properties
  • Analytics: Built-in reporting provides faster insights into campaign performance, enabling data-driven adjustments
40% reduction in time spent on social media management

Viva Engage (Communities) - Internal Knowledge Sharing

  • Information Access: Reduces time searching email threads; centralizes institutional knowledge in searchable communities
  • Cross-Functional Collaboration: AI Task Force and other communities enable faster problem-solving and best practice sharing
  • Onboarding: New employees access historical context and resources without needing to ask repetitive questions
  • Status: Initial communities launched; broader rollout pending governance alignment
Estimated 20-30% reduction in time searching for information

Current Pilots - In Progress

Sage Intacct - Construction Accounting Modernization

Problem Solved: Current Timberline system causes change order delays and errors; change orders sent via email to coordinators often get lost, creating project tracking gaps and financial reconciliation issues.

  • Workflow Improvement: Sage Intacct consolidates change order management into a single streamlined workflow, eliminating email-based handoffs
  • Accuracy: Real-time visibility into project financials reduces month-end surprises and improves budget vs. actual tracking
  • Reporting: Standardized construction accounting reports enable faster, more accurate financial analysis for leadership
  • Timeline: Implementation started in 2025; full rollout continues into 2026
Expected to reduce change order processing time by 50-60%

Procore - Construction Project Management Enhancement

Current Status: Limited progress due to data/process readiness requirements; newer AI-enabled modules available but not yet piloted.

  • Field Workflows: Improved document control, RFI tracking, and punch list management reduce manual steps and project delays
  • Collaboration: Centralized platform for contractor, architect, and internal team communication eliminates version control issues
  • AI Features: Newer modules offer predictive scheduling, automated document extraction, and risk identification capabilities
  • 2026 Plan: Confirm module scope, ensure data/process readiness, then pilot AI features intentionally

2026 Strategic Priorities

Scale Core AI Platforms (Primary Goal)

  • ChatGPT Business → Enterprise: Standardize enterprise-grade governance, security, and reusable custom assistants across departments
  • Microsoft 365 Copilot Expansion: Deploy licensed seats where ROI is clear; develop role-based training; explore agent/workflow automation (Copilot Studio)
  • Governance Framework: Establish "approved use" guidelines, curated assistant library, and usage policy alignment

Department-Specific Tool Expansion

  • Legal: Spellbook (contract drafting/review); evaluate Kira, Luminance, eBrevia, Leverton, ThoughtRiver for document comparison
  • Accounting: Yardi add-ons for AP processing, recurring entries, and enhanced reporting to accelerate monthly close
  • Resident Experience: Evaluate Tulu/Elevate for amenity management; expand smart building pilot (Randolph thermostats) based on results

Systems Foundation (Enablers for Future AI/Automation)

  • Yardi 7S → Voyager 8 Upgrade: Platform modernization improves reporting readiness and enables future AI add-ons
  • Procore Module Expansion: Complete data/process readiness work to unlock AI-enabled features
  • Docutrax: Complete Commercial/Industrial rollout; define ongoing reporting and ownership
