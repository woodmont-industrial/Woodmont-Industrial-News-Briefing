Which One Should You Choose?

Choose Copilot Free if:

You want basic AI assistance for personal learning, brainstorming, or casual use. You don't need to access company files or work with sensitive business data.

Choose Microsoft 365 Copilot (Licensed) if:

Your work involves company data, workflows, or reporting. You need AI embedded directly in Word, Excel, Outlook, and Teams. You require enterprise-grade security and compliance. You want to boost productivity at scale across your organization.
